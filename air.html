✈️ Air Transport

Explore the skies with planes, helicopters, drones and more. Tap any card to learn about the vehicle and its manufacturers.

✈️ Aeroplane (Jet)

Large aircraft used for carrying passengers across long distances.

🛩️ Private Jet

Smaller jet planes used for private or business travel.

🚁 Helicopter

Can take off vertically and hover in place — used for rescue and transport.

🎈 Hot Air Balloon

An air vehicle that floats using heated air inside a large balloon.

🪂 Glider

An unpowered aircraft that soars through the sky using wind currents.

🛸 Drone

Remote-controlled aircraft used for photography, delivery, and fun!

🎈 Blimp (Airship)

Lighter-than-air craft that floats using helium or hot gas.

🪂 Hang Glider

A lightweight, foot-launched glider flown for sport or adventure.

🪂 Paraglider

A parachute-like aircraft for soaring through the air.

📦 Cargo Aircraft

Large planes designed for carrying goods instead of people.

🛶 Seaplane

Aircraft that can take off and land on water.

🛩️ Fighter Jet

High-speed military aircraft built for combat and defence missions.

🚀 Space Shuttle

A reusable spacecraft used to reach low Earth orbit and return safely.
