🚆 Rail Transport

Vehicles that travel on tracks — helping people and goods move across cities and countries.

🚆 Train

A classic rail vehicle for carrying passengers or cargo long distances.

🚄 Bullet Train (Shinkansen)

Ultra-fast trains found in countries like Japan and China.

🚇 Metro / Underground

Trains that run under cities to move people quickly without traffic.

🚊 Tram

Smaller rail vehicle that runs on streets — often in city centres.

🚈 Light Rail

Faster than a tram but lighter than a train — ideal for suburbs.

🚝 Monorail

A rail system with only one track — often seen in airports and theme parks.

🚛 Freight Train

Carries goods like coal, food, and containers across long distances.

🧲 Maglev Train

Floats on magnets to reach incredible speeds with no wheels!

⛰️ Funicular Railway

A cable-based railway that climbs steep hills or mountains.
