🚢 Water Transport

Explore all the amazing ways people travel across and under water!

🛶 Boat

A small watercraft used on rivers, lakes, and near shores.

🚢 Ship

Large sea vessel used for cargo or passengers across oceans.

📦 Cargo Ship

Massive ships designed to carry goods in containers worldwide.

🛥️ Yacht

A luxurious boat for cruising and private leisure trips.

⛴️ Ferry

Used to carry people, vehicles, or goods across short water routes.

🚤 Speedboat

Fast-moving boat used for racing, water sports, or patrol.

🛳️ Cruise Ship

Floating hotel offering long-distance holiday voyages.

🤿 Submarine

An underwater vessel used for research, military, or deep-sea exploration.

🌊 Jet Ski

A fast personal watercraft that you ride like a motorbike on water.

🛶 Canoe

Narrow, human-powered boat used with paddles in lakes or rivers.

🚣 Kayak

A small, covered boat paddled using a double-bladed oar.

⛵ Sailboat

A boat powered by the wind using sails — used for sport or travel.

🎣 Fishing Trawler

A work boat used to catch fish at sea — big nets and strong engines!

🌀 Hovercraft

A craft that glides above water (or land!) using air cushions.

🛢️ Oil Tanker

Massive ships that carry oil and fuel across oceans.
