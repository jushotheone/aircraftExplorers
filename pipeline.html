🛢️ Pipeline Transport

Pipelines silently move important materials like oil, water, and gas over great distances — all underground or underwater!

🛢️ Crude Oil Pipeline

Used to transport unprocessed oil from wells to refineries.

🔥 Natural Gas Pipeline

Carries natural gas to homes, factories, and power stations.

🚰 Water Pipeline

Transports clean water from treatment plants to homes and buildings.

💩 Sewage Pipeline

Moves wastewater from homes and toilets to treatment facilities.

🥛 Milk Pipeline

Pipes inside dairies that move milk for cooling, processing, and packaging.

⛏️ Slurry Pipeline

Moves thick liquid mixtures like coal or ore in mining operations.

💨 Compressed Air Pipeline

Used in factories to deliver air power to machines and tools.
