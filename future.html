🌀 Future Transport

Explore the wild, high-tech and futuristic ways we might travel in the future. Some already exist — others are just around the corner!

🌀 Hyperloop Pod

Super-fast pods that travel through vacuum tubes — almost as fast as planes!

🚗✨ Flying Car

A car that can take off and fly — combining driving and flying in one!

🚁 Drone Taxi

A self-flying passenger drone — no driver, just press a button and fly!

🧲 Advanced Maglev

Floating trains using powerful magnets for ultra-high speed travel.

🎒 Jetpack

A wearable flying device that lets one person fly like a superhero!

🚄 Vacuum Tube Train

Trains that move through sealed tubes with almost no air — ultra-fast and quiet.

🛩️ Hydrogen Aircraft

Zero-emission planes powered by clean hydrogen fuel.

🤖 Self-driving Pods

Tiny smart vehicles that drive themselves — no steering wheel needed!

🌌 Space Elevator

A futuristic lift that goes from Earth all the way into space — still science fiction for now!
