🚡 Cable Transport

Vehicles that move using overhead or underground cables — perfect for mountains, ski resorts, and city hills!

🚡 Cable Car

Enclosed cabin moved by overhead cables, often used in cities or mountains.

🚠 Gondola Lift

Series of cable cars used for transporting people up ski slopes or steep terrains.

🎿 Chairlift

Open seats that carry skiers or hikers up mountains using cables.

🪢 Ropeway

A general term for any transport system that uses ropes or cables to move loads or people.

🚟 Aerial Tramway

Large cabin pulled by cables, usually with two cabins going opposite directions.

⛰️ Funicular

Two rail cars on a steep hill, pulled by cables and counterbalanced.

🧗 Zipline

A fun ride where a person or item glides down a stretched cable using gravity.
