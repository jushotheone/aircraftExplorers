🚗 Road Transport

Explore all the amazing vehicles that move on roads around the world.

🚗 Car

The most common road vehicle for personal transport.

🚌 Bus

Large vehicle for carrying many passengers along a route.

🚌 Coach

Comfortable long-distance bus used for travel or tours.

🚕 Taxi

A vehicle available for hire with a driver.

🚐 Van

Often used for transporting goods or small groups of people.

🚛 Lorry / Truck

Heavy-duty vehicle for transporting large loads.

🛻 Pickup Truck

Smaller truck with open cargo space at the back.

🏍️ Motorcycle

A two-wheeled motor vehicle, great for speed and flexibility.

🛵 Scooter

A small motorised vehicle ideal for city commuting.

🚲 Bicycle

Pedal-powered vehicle that's eco-friendly and healthy.

🛴 Electric Scooter

Battery-powered scooter for short-distance rides.

🚐 Campervan / RV

A home on wheels for camping and travel adventures.

🚒 Fire Engine

A specialised truck used by firefighters to put out fires.

🚑 Ambulance

Emergency vehicle for transporting injured or sick people.

🚓 Police Car

A vehicle used by the police to patrol and respond to emergencies.

🚜 Tractor

A powerful vehicle used on farms to pull equipment and carry loads.

🛺 Rickshaw

Three-wheeled vehicle used widely for short city trips.

🛺 Tuk-tuk

A motorised version of a rickshaw — common in Asia and Africa.

🛹 Skateboard

A small board with wheels used for sport and casual movement.

🤖 Segway

A self-balancing personal transporter powered by electricity.
